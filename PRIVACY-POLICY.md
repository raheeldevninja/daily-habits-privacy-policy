# Privacy Policy – DailyHabits

**Effective date:** March 15, 2026  
**Last updated:** March 15, 2026

DailyHabits ("we", "us", "our") is a simple offline habit tracker mobile application developed by Raheel.

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (or rather — do **not** collect) when you use DailyHabits.

## 1. Information We Collect

DailyHabits is a **completely offline application**. We do **not** collect, store, transmit or share any personal information.

- No account creation is required  
- No name, email, phone number, age, gender or any other personal identifiers are asked  
- No location data is collected  
- No device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, etc.) are read or sent anywhere  
- No usage analytics, crash reports or tracking SDKs are included  
- All habit data is stored **only locally** on your device using secure on-device storage  

## 2. Third-Party Services

DailyHabits does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks or cloud providers.

## 3. Children's Privacy

DailyHabits is **not** directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The new version will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
